TCC Childcare Scholarship: New Enrollment 2018-2019

About the Scholarship

MIT offers a scholarship to eligible Institute employees, including postdoctoral associates and postdoctoral fellows, who have a child or children enrolled in one of MIT’s Technology Childcare Centers (TCC). To estimate the potential amount of your scholarship please visit the Award Calculator webpage.

How to Apply

  1. Read the Childcare Scholarship Program Fact Sheet
  2. Check the box below to indicate your child has been offered a space at a TCC. This action will make the Apply for Childcare Scholarship buttons appear.
  3. Click an Apply for Childcare Scholarship button to open up the enrollment form.
